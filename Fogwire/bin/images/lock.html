Locking Instructions

Step 1:    From the Lock Panel, Browse for data to lock or Enter Text to Lock
  1. Type your message directly into the text area, or to browse for a file, click the browse button and select a text file.
  2. Click Open to display the text file in the Locked Text area.
  3. If you are sending locked text to another registered FogWire user, enter the user's email address into the To field.


Step 2:   Choose a FogCode


  1. Decide on a fogcode that you will use to lock your document.
  2. If you are exchanging data with another registerd FogWire user, communicate this fogcode to that user.
  3. Click the New FogCode button to change the fogcode.


Step 3:   Click the Lock & Send button or Click Lock
  1. After you have chosen your fogcode, decide whether or not to send the locked data to another registered FogWire user or to simply save the locked data on your computer.
  2. Click the lock button to lock the text and save a copy of the data into the locked.txt file
  3. The locked file is saved to: C:\FogWire2\Locked_Docs\locked.txt


IMPORTANT NOTES:


  1. The folders: C:\FogWire2\Unlocked_Docs and C:\FogWire2\Locked_Docs and the files: C:\FogWire2\Unlocked_Docs\unlocked.txt and C:\FogWire2\Locked_Docs\locked.txt are required by FogWire.
  2. locked.txt and unlocked.txt are overwritten each time you lock data.
  3. Locked documents can only be unlocked with the correct fogcode.
  4. Please allow for appropriate time to lock text files larger than 1MB.
  5. Fogwire only sends encrypted files to other registered Fogwire users.
  6. Locking and sending a previously locked document may make the data unusable.
